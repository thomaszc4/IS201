Overview

The Piano Guys is a musical group that blends classical music with pop culture, and their unique sound has garnered them a massive following. The group consists of four members: Jon Schmidt (pianist), Steven Sharp Nelson (cellist), Al Van der Beek (music producer), and Paul Anderson (videographer). The group was formed in 2010 in Utah, USA, and gained their initial popularity by uploading videos of their music performances to YouTube. The group's early videos were filmed in stunning natural locations, which helped them gain millions of views and followers.

The Piano Guys Now

Today, The Piano Guys are a well-established and successful musical group. They have released several albums, including their self-titled debut album in 2012, followed by several more albums and EPs. The group's popularity has only increased over time, with sold-out concerts around the world and over 6 million subscribers on their YouTube channel. The group's music has also been used in several movies, TV shows, and commercials, further solidifying their place in the music industry.
